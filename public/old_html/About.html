About Us

Who We Are

We are Tutors To Go, the newest tutoring service in the Penn State and State College area.

What We Offer

As a tutoring service with no central location, we have the unique ability of being able to come to you at any location on Penn State's University Park campus. In addition, our tutors can come to select off-campus locations. We also offer course packets and review packets for many courses. Still not satisfied? Email us! We are always trying to improve and expand, so we would be glad to work with you in tutoring a new class, working at a new location, or offering new services!

Why Us?

With so many other tutoring services offered to Penn State students, you may be wondering why you should choose us. Unlike our competitors, our tutors will come to you with no time limits in how long we can help you. This is especially helpful during the cold winter months that you have come to know and love. In addition, this allows us to be incredibly flexible in our tutors' hours, as tutors will often be available late into the night and even 24/7. All of our tutors are students who have done very well in these courses in recent years, so they know what to expect on homework assignments and exams.


To Summarize:

  • We come to you
  • Easy to use website and app interface
  • No time limits
  • Affordable offerings
  • Tutors who know the course material and how it is taught
